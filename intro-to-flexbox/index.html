Draupnir Spear

The Draupnir Spear is a new weapon added to Kratos' arsenal during the Path Quest "Forging Destiny." In this quest, Kratos, Freya, and Brok visit the Lady of the Forge in Svartalheim. The Lady crafts the Spear for Kratos, which unlocks new abilities and skills. The Spear is contained in a ring that Kratos wears, and when in use can do a great amount of range and melee damage. It can also self-replicate, meaning you can throw multiple spears at your targets.

You'll also be able to use the Spear to access areas you haven't been able to get to before. There are marks in stones that, when you throw the spear at it, create a step you can climb. The Spear can also destroy rocks and obstacles. When multiple spears are thrown at a target, press (Triangle), and the spears will detonate, destroying the rocks and clearing a path.

Group #2 Heading

Blades of Chaos

The Blades of Chaos are a staple of the God of War franchise and a constant reminder of Kratos' past. These powerful blades are bound to his wrists and can deal a massive amount of damage to hordes of enemies in a matter of seconds

Shatter Star Shield

The Shatter Star Shield is a long-range version of the shield. Acting like the Dauntless shield and being a high-risk, high-reward option. Instead of the usual parry, if you double tap L1, you will perform a shield punch which blows enemies backwards.

Group #3 Heading

Leviathan Axe

The Leviathan Axe is a new addition to the Nordic era of God of War and becomes Kratos' main offensive weapon in the games. This powerful weapon is enchanted with the power of ice and can be summoned from any distance.

Guardian Shield

Kratos also has defense weapons, like the Guardian Shield, which is retractable and can shield him from upcoming damage.
